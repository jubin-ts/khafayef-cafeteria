About Us

A Taste of Authentic Arabic Flavors

Khafayef Cafeteria brings together the rich culinary traditions of the Middle East and beyond. From our signature Lebanese Mix Grill Platters cooked over charcoal to fragrant Mandi rice, crispy Broasted chicken, and delicate Kunafa desserts — every dish tells a story of passion and authenticity.

Located in Al Yasmeen, Ajman, we proudly serve customers across Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman & Al Ain. Available on Talabat for convenient delivery.
