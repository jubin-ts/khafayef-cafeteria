About Us

A Taste of Authentic Arabic Flavors in Al Yasmeen, Ajman

Khafayef Cafeteria & Restaurant brings together the rich culinary traditions of the Middle East and beyond. From our signature Lebanese Mix Grill Platters cooked over charcoal to fragrant Mandi rice, crispy Broasted chicken, and delicate Kunafa desserts — every dish tells a story of passion and authenticity.

Located in Al Yasmeen, Ajman, we are the go-to restaurant for families and food lovers across the Emirates. Order conveniently on Talabat or Noon Food, or call us for direct delivery to Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman & Al Ain.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Khafayef Cafeteria & Restaurant located?

Khafayef Cafeteria & Restaurant is located in Al Yasmeen, Ajman, UAE. We are easily accessible from Sharjah, Dubai and Al Ain. Get directions on Google Maps.

What are the opening hours?

We are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — we never close! Visit us anytime, day or night.

How can I order food from Khafayef online?

You can order through Talabat, Noon Food, WhatsApp, or call us at 050 799 0910.

Does Khafayef deliver to Dubai, Sharjah and Al Ain?

Yes! We deliver across Ajman, Sharjah, Dubai and Al Ain through Talabat, Noon Food and direct delivery. Free delivery on orders above AED 50.

What type of food does Khafayef serve?

We serve over 200 dishes including Arabic charcoal Mix Grills, Mandi Rice, Shawarma, Broasted Chicken, Biriyani, Chinese food, Indian curries, burgers, desserts like Kunafa, and fresh juices. All food is 100% Halal.

Is Khafayef Cafeteria Halal certified?

Yes, Khafayef Cafeteria & Restaurant serves 100% Halal food. All our meat is sourced from certified Halal suppliers.
