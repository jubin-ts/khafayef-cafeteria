Top 5 Must-Try Charcoal Grilled Dishes at Khafayef, Al Yasmeen Ajman

There's nothing quite like the smoky aroma of fresh meat sizzling over hot charcoal. At Khafayef Cafeteria & Restaurant in Al Yasmeen, Ajman, charcoal grilling is more than a cooking method — it's an art form passed down through generations of Arabic culinary tradition.

Here are our top 5 charcoal grilled dishes you absolutely must try:

  1. Khafayef Platter (AED 150) — Our signature masterpiece. A feast of Mutton Tikka, Meat Kebab, Lamb Chops, Arayes, Shish Tawook, Chicken Kebab, and Wings. Served with Lebanese bread, Hummus, Fattoush salad, and garlic sauce. Perfect for sharing with family and friends.
  2. Arabic Chicken Charcoal (AED 37) — Whole chicken marinated in our secret Arabic spice blend, slow-grilled over natural charcoal until golden and juicy. A true taste of the Gulf.
  1. Mutton Tikka (AED 28) — Tender pieces of premium mutton, marinated overnight in yogurt and spices, then grilled to perfection on charcoal skewers.
  2. Shish Tawook (AED 20) — Classic Lebanese chicken skewers, marinated in garlic, lemon, and sumac. Light, flavourful, and a customer favourite.
  3. Grilled Sea Bream (AED 45) — Fresh whole sea bream, seasoned with herbs and lemon, grilled over charcoal. A lighter option that's full of flavour.

All our charcoal grilled items are made to order and served fresh. Visit us at Al Yasmeen, Ajman or order on Talabat and Noon Food. We're open 24/7!

Best Mandi Rice in Ajman: Why Khafayef's Charcoal Mandi Stands Out

If you're searching for the best Mandi rice in Ajman, look no further than Khafayef Cafeteria & Restaurant in Al Yasmeen. While there are many Mandi restaurants across Ajman — including Rahat Al Mandi and others — Khafayef's Mandi has earned a loyal following for one key reason: we cook our Mandi chicken over real charcoal.

Unlike conventional oven-cooked Mandi, our charcoal method infuses the meat with a distinctive smoky flavour while keeping it incredibly tender. The rice is cooked with traditional Mandi spices — cardamom, saffron, dried limes — absorbing all the aromatic flavours.

Our Mandi Rice Options:

  • Chicken Mandi — AED 24 (our bestseller)
  • Mutton Mandi — AED 45
  • Lamb Full Mandi — AED 500 (feeds 8-10 people, perfect for gatherings)
  • Zurbian Chicken/Mutton — Spicier version for those who love heat
  • Madfoon Chicken — Traditional slow-cooked preparation

We also serve Bukhari rice, Biriyani, and Mazbi varieties. Whether you're a family in Al Yasmeen looking for dinner, or ordering delivery to Sharjah, Dubai, or Al Ain — Khafayef's Mandi is just a call or tap away.
